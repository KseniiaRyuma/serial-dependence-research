Thank you for your participation! This experiment is about orientation perception. In each trial, a patch of dark and bright stripes will be displayed at a certain orientation for 0.5 seconds. Please pay attention to the orientation of the stripes. In each trial, after the stripe patch is displayed a red circle will appear at the center of the screen. Please move your mouse cursor onto the red circle and then the circle will turn into a green circle with a black bar. Then please move the mouse to adjust the orientation of the bar so that it looks identical to the orientation of the stripe patch that you have just seen. After you have matched the orientation, you can click the left mouse key to proceed to the next trial.

If occasionally you missed the display of the stripe patch, instead of making a random guess, please click the button "Yes, unfortunately I missed the display of the arrow" to redo that trial.
